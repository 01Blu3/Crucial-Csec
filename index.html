Phishing is a type of attack that is used in order to steal peoples data, that data ranges from login details to credit card numbers. These occur when an attacker tricks a victim into believing that the attacker is a trustworthy source and gets the victim to cick on a malicious link, which can lead to malware infection which in turn can lead to further escalations.

Password Entropy

Password entropy is the measurement of how unpredictable a Password is, it is based on the different types of characters used ranging from numbers, letters and symbols. What this method does is estimate how difficult it would be to crack a given password. While the ideal password lengths might not be completely practical, it does provide a guiding line.
